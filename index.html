About Us

Pragati College of Arts & Commerce is situated in Thane in Maharashtra state of India. Established in 1997, it is accredited from NAAC and it is affiliated to University of Mumbai. PCAC, Thane offers 19 courses across 6 streams namely Commerce and Banking, Arts, Science, Management, IT, Vocational and across 8 degrees like BA, B.Com, BSc, BMS, MA.Hostel facility is not available for its students. Additional campus facilities such as Auditorium, Canteen, Computer Lab, Gym, Library, Cls. Room are also there.
